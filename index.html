Is keeping a diary or a bullet journal too much work for you, but you'd still like to record how your day went? DailyTracker can help!

DailyTracker is an app that will let you record your dailies. You can record the hours you've slept, the activities you did, and keep track of your moods. You can even set a long-term and short-term goal for yourself, to help keep yourself on track.

DailyTracker will use your recorded results, and analyze how you're doing over time. You can see a graph of how well you've been sleeping each night, and how often you've recorded activities and moods. DailyTracker will take this information, and be a support system for you, encouraging you to keep up with the good work (really!).
